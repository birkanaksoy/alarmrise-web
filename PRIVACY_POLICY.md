# Privacy Policy — AlarmRise

**Last updated:** June 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AlarmRise ("we", "the app") processes your personal data when you use our iOS application and services ("the Service").

Governing law: Republic of Turkey **Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK, Law No. 6698)** and Apple App Store guidelines.

---

## 1. Data Controller

Birkan Aksoy
Email: abirkan.aksoy@gmail.com

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Data Stored On-Device (never sent to us)
- Alarm times, labels, mission selections
- Sleep session records (start/end, motion, audio peaks)
- Snore detection summary (raw audio is **not stored**)
- Health data (written to Apple Health via HealthKit, with your permission)
- Preferences (theme, language, settings)

This data syncs **only between your own devices** via iCloud (CloudKit for Pro users). We cannot see it.

### 2.2 Anonymous Analytics
- Total alarm count, successful wake rate, session count
- Device model, iOS version, app version
- **No personal identifiers.** Collected anonymously via Apple App Analytics.

### 2.3 Support Requests
Messages sent through the support form are delivered to `abirkan.aksoy@gmail.com`. They are kept only to resolve your request and are never shared with third parties.

### 2.4 Subscriptions & Purchases
Handled by Apple App Store. Payment information stays with Apple — we **never see it**.

## 3. Data We Do Not Collect

- Location (used on-device for local weather only, never transmitted)
- Microphone recordings (only real-time peak analysis; raw audio is discarded)
- Contacts, photos, calendar contents
- IP address, cookies, advertising ID

## 4. Permissions

| Permission | Purpose | Required? |
|---|---|---|
| Notifications | To ring the alarm on time | **Yes** |
| Microphone | Sleep & snore analysis (on-device) | Optional |
| Motion | Shake mission and sleep stages | Optional |
| Calendar | Smart wake-up based on meetings | Optional |
| Health | Write sleep data to Apple Health | Optional |
| Proximity / Location | Weather (on-device) | Optional |

You can change permissions at any time from iOS Settings → AlarmRise.

## 5. Third-Party Services

- **Apple iCloud / CloudKit** — syncs Pro users' data on Apple's infrastructure, **with no access by us**.
- **Apple HealthKit / WeatherKit / MusicKit** — subject to Apple's privacy policies.

There are **no** third-party ad networks, tracking SDKs, or social media integrations.

## 6. Data Retention

Data on your device is kept **until you delete it**. Use Settings → "Delete All Data" to remove everything. Your iCloud data is wiped as part of the same action.

## 7. Your Rights Under KVKK (Article 11)

Under KVKK you have the right to:

- Learn whether your personal data is being processed
- Request information about how it is processed
- Learn the purpose of processing and whether the data is used in line with that purpose
- Request correction or deletion (you can do this directly via Settings → "Delete All Data")
- Export your data in a portable format (Settings → "Download My Data")
- Claim compensation if you suffer damages

To exercise these rights, write to `abirkan.aksoy@gmail.com`.

## 8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to users under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.
