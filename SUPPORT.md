## 🙋 Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the alarm ring in silent mode?
**We're currently waiting on Critical Alerts approval.** Until that comes through, for the alarm to ring make sure:
- **The side switch is not set to silent**
- **Focus / DND mode is off**
- **Notification permission is granted**

The in-app "Before Bed" checklist verifies these automatically.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
iOS Settings → Apple ID → **Subscriptions** → AlarmRise → **Cancel**

Or from inside the app: Settings → About → **Manage Subscription**

### How long is the free trial?
If you choose the yearly plan, you get a **7-day free trial**. Cancel within that window and you won't be charged.

### Which devices sync my data?
For Pro subscribers, your data syncs automatically across all your Apple devices via iCloud. Your data **never touches our servers** — it stays in your own iCloud account.

### Can I delete my data?
Yes. Settings → Account → **Delete All Data** wipes everything from both your device and iCloud.

### Does it work on Apple Watch?
Yes. With the Watch app you can view, dismiss and snooze alarms. Requires watchOS 11+.

### iCloud sync isn't working
1. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID
2. Check Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → make sure AlarmRise is enabled
3. Your Pro subscription must be active

### The app doesn't open when the alarm goes off
The app may not be active in the background. Don't force-quit it (don't swipe it away from the app switcher). Use the in-app **Before Bed Checklist**.

### I want a refund
Apple's refund policy applies. Submit a request at [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com).
